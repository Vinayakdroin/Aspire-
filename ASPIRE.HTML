Goethe Institute Syllabus (A1 to B2)

📌 A1 Level – Beginner

Goal: Understand and use everyday expressions, introduce yourself, and interact in simple conversations.

🔹 Topics Covered:

  • Introducing yourself and others
  • Asking for and giving basic personal information
  • Ordering food and drinks
  • Shopping and prices
  • Telling time and dates

📌 A2 Level – Elementary

Goal: Communicate in routine situations, describe personal experiences, and handle common social interactions.

🔹 Topics Covered:

  • Talking about past events & experiences
  • Expressing preferences and opinions
  • Making appointments, giving directions
  • Travel and transport
  • Health and emergencies

📌 B1 Level – Intermediate

Goal: Express thoughts clearly on familiar topics, understand media content, and handle everyday work-life interactions.

🔹 Topics Covered:

  • Giving opinions and arguments
  • Talking about future plans and goals
  • Understanding and discussing news and current events
  • Writing formal emails and letters
  • Workplace communication and interviews

📌 B2 Level – Upper Intermediate

Goal: Speak fluently in discussions, understand complex texts, and express detailed viewpoints in professional settings.

🔹 Topics Covered:

  • Formal and business communication
  • Debating and defending viewpoints
  • Writing detailed reports and articles
  • Analyzing literature and media
  • Understanding spoken German in various dialects
